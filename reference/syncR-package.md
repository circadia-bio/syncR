# syncR: Integrate and Coordinate Data Across the Circadia Lab Package Ecosystem

syncR is the integrator and coordinator of the Circadia Lab R ecosystem.
It pulls sociodemographic and questionnaire data from tallieR, sleep
diary data from slumbR, and actigraphy-derived circadian metrics from
zeitR into a single unified, participant-indexed database. syncR::sync()
— pulling everything into alignment, just like the SCN does.
